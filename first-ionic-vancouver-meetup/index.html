First Ionic Vancouver Meetup

Ever since creating an account on meetup.com way back in January, I haven't had the time to take a good look at the events held by the various tech groups located around Vancouver.

It's only a week ago, that I got to know of an event hosted by the Ionic Vancouver Meetup Group through my email subscription from meetup.com.

Since I had no clue was Ionic is about, I was pretty interested in the event as the hosts were planning to give an introduction to the Ionic Framework & share some of their challenges that they encountered through developing hybrid apps with it. It was an obvious decision to spend my night attending the event instead of studying for my MATH220 final xD.

Takeaways from the presentations:

  • Ionic's Ideology: "Develop once, deploy everywhere"
  • i.e. You can make apps for both iOS & Android from a single JavaScript codebase
  • You can access a smartphone native functionalities (Camera,bluetooth etc.) through ngCordova. Mind Blown =0
  • Don't use Ionic if your app is going to be graphically intensive (Lots of animation, 3D games etc.)
  • Ionic 2 is going to be more awesome =D

Anyways, I was quite surprised to see little to none other students attending the event (I think I was the only kid in the room that's still schooling LOL) but I still managed to network with a few Chinese startup founders & developers at the end.

Overall, the event was extremely rewarding (compared to staying at home to study for MATH220) & I see a huge potential in the future of hybrid apps. So I've decided to focus on picking up Ionic & some more Rails/Node.js instead of learning Swift this summer.

Definitely looking forward for the next Ionic meetup!
